Meat Candy

Meat Candy(Steak)

Ingredients

  • 1 pinch cayenne pepper
  • 2 cups brown sugar, or as needed
  • 2 (14 ounce) packages little smoked sausages
  • 1 (16 ounce) package bacon slices, cut into thirds (or as needed)
  • 1 box toothpicks

Preparation Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Line a large baking dish with aluminum foil
  2. Stir cayenne pepper into brown sugar in a bowl. Wrap each smoked sausage in a piece of bacon; secure each with a toothpick. Arrange wrapped sausages into prepared baking dish. Pack brown sugar around wrapped sausages to cover by about 1/2 inch. Cover pan tightly with aluminum foil.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven for 20 minutes. Increase oven heat to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C). Remove aluminum foil from pan.
  4. Continue baking in the hot oven until brown sugar has melted and thickened, 30 to 40 minutes.
